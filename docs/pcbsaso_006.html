A sampling of self-organisation in CA

  • John von Neumann (+Stanislaw Ulam) and John Horton Conway develop CA and the Game of Life with replication, construction, and complexity in mind ().
  • Richard Guy discovers the reflex glider in Life (~1970)
  • Stephan Rafler describes a glider in SmoothLife (2014)
  • Bert Chan develops an expansive taxonomy of solitons in Lenia (2019, web)
  • Growing neural cellular automata (Mordvintsev et al. 2020), Asymptotic Lenia (Kawaguchi et al. 2021), Glaberish (Davis et al.2022), and many more recent developments.
